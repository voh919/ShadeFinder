About Us

We're Vivian, Sarah, and Mia, and we're always on the hunt for the greatest beauty products.

There's a popular TikTok trend where people post pictures of everyday objects and ask for product recommendations that match the shade. We thought, why not digitize this idea and make sure everyone can find their perfect product easily and instantly?

With ShadeFinder, you can upload any image, and our system will help you discover the best beauty products that match the colors you love.
